Education · Children

Empowering You

ASIF Foundation has partnered with Thảo Đàn Social Enterprise — an organisation with over 30 years of experience in child protection and development — to launch this initiative. Designed as a holistic solution, the project builds a sustainable ecosystem where vulnerable children don’t just stay in school, but flourish intellectually, emotionally, and socially. Because every child deserves to learn, to laugh, and to dream.

Healthcare · Chronic Patients

Living Well with Dialysis

Vietnam’s first comprehensive care model for chronic kidney disease patients on dialysis in Ho Chi Minh City — addressing nutrition, mental health, spiritual wellbeing, and social reintegration. Because surviving is not enough; these patients deserve to live with dignity, connection, and love.

240+ Patients supported
1st Model of its kind in Vietnam
